is it written
in the stars?

By Namu Sampath

What's your sign? What does your chart tell someone about you? What job are you likely to have based on your zodiac sign? These are all questions that are trending on social media. According to the Western zodiac, there are 12 signs: Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces. Each have their own personality traits, symbols, compatibilities and more.

Something I've found very interesting is the overlap between personality traits and zodiac signs. For example, I am a Cancer. A top search result for Cancers is that we are "loyal to the end." For this project, I wanted to see how people truly see themselves, and whether there are any connections between personality traits and zodiac signs.

This is a scrolly (scrolling+story). Start scrolling to read.

I asked people to provide their birthday, gender, age group, to choose the four personality traits they most identify with, and to identify their profession for a survey to curate this dataset.

In this sunburst (no pun intended) graphic, we can see each zodiac sign and the personality trait they most closely identified with. Three signs: Aries, Cancer, and Leo responded with 'loyal' as their top choice. Similarly, three signs: Gemini, Virgo and Scorpio, responded with 'curious' as their top choice.

The sign that responded the most to the survey was Leo, with 14 responses. However, water signs as a group (i.e. Cancers, Scorpios, and Pisces) had the most number of responses overall, with 28 responses. In total, 104 people responded to the survey.

According to the survey, 36 people work in a STEM-related profession, whereas 15 people identify as journalists. Because of the small sample size, only two of the 12 signs had non-STEM-related responses as their top career choice: Aquarius (Communications) and Virgo (students).
